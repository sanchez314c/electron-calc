# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants. Our community is built on respect, kindness, and collaboration.

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful**: Treat others with dignity and respect
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- **Be collaborative**: Work together to solve problems
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be patient**: Remember that everyone has different skill levels
- **Be professional**: Maintain a professional and courteous demeanor

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- **Spam**: Unwanted promotional content, off-topic posts
- **Trolling**: Deliberate provocation or disruption
- **Personal attacks**: Insults, ad hominem arguments, or personal criticism
- **Privacy violations**: Sharing private information without consent
- **Threats**: Any form of intimidation or threat

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

### How to Report

1. **Email**: conduct@superclaude.ai
2. **GitHub**: Report to any project maintainer
3. **Private Message**: Send a private message to moderators

### What to Include

- Your contact information (optional)
- Description of the incident
- Parties involved
- Date and time of incident
- Any available evidence (screenshots, links)

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: Within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: Within 3 business days
3. **Resolution**: Within 7 business days
4. **Follow-up**: As needed

## Enforcement Guidelines

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct.

### Consequences

- **Warning**: Private warning for minor violations
- **Temporary Ban**: 24-72 hour ban for repeated violations
- **Permanent Ban**: For severe violations or repeated offenses
- **Legal Action**: For illegal activities or threats

### Appeal Process

If you believe a consequence is unfair:

1. **Wait 24 hours** to cool down
2. **Email**: appeal@superclaude.ai
3. **Include**: Original incident, consequence, and why you believe it's unfair
4. **Review**: Different maintainer will review within 5 business days

## Community Guidelines

### Communication

- **Language**: English is preferred, but we welcome all languages
- **Tone**: Keep it positive and constructive
- **Feedback**: Focus on code, not the person
- **Questions**: There are no stupid questions - ask away!

### Contributions

- **First-time contributors**: We especially welcome first-time contributors
- **Code reviews**: Be kind and helpful in reviews
- **Documentation**: Help improve docs for everyone
- **Issues**: Search before creating new issues

### Online Meetings

- **Recording**: Always announce if recording
- **Chat**: Keep chat relevant and respectful
- **Questions**: Use raise hand feature or wait for Q&A
- **Background**: Be mindful of your background if on video

## Diversity and Inclusion

We are committed to creating an inclusive community:

- **Accessibility**: Ensure all content is accessible
- **Time zones**: Schedule meetings for different time zones
- **Language**: Be patient with non-native English speakers
- **Culture**: Respect cultural differences and holidays

## Positive Recognition

We celebrate positive behavior:

- **Contributor of the Month**: Recognize outstanding contributors
- **Helpful Awards**: Acknowledge those who help others
- **Mentor Recognition**: Thank those who mentor newcomers
- **Community Champion**: Recognize inclusive behavior

## Contact Information

### Code of Conduct Committee

- **Email**: conduct@superclaude.ai
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours
- **Confidentiality**: All reports are confidential

### Emergency Contacts

For immediate threats or illegal activities:

- **Email**: emergency@superclaude.ai
- **Law Enforcement**: We will cooperate with law enforcement as needed

## Acknowledgment

By participating in this community, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
